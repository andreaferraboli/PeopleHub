PeopleHub — Logo prompts for AI image generators

Brand context (give the generator this if it helps):
  - App: "PeopleHub", a private, offline personal relationship manager. Themes:
    people, connection, the people "circle", birthdays, staying in touch.
  - Identity: "Midnight Gold" — neo-luxury, dark-first, editorial.
  - Colors: onyx background #131313, champagne-gold accent #f2ca50.
  - Type feel: editorial serif (Bodoni Moda) for any lettering.
  - Want: a clean, scalable app icon / brand mark that reads well at 48px.

How to use:
  - Works with Midjourney, DALL·E 3, Stable Diffusion, Ideogram, etc.
  - For Midjourney, append parameters like: --v 6 --ar 1:1 --style raw
  - Ideogram / DALL·E handle text/lettering best if you need a real "P" or "PH".
  - Most prompts assume a square (1:1) icon. Ask for "on a transparent
    background" or "flat color background #131313" depending on your need.
  - General negative prompt (SD): photorealistic, 3d render, drop shadow, gradient
    mesh, cluttered, busy, text artifacts, watermark, stock-photo people, clip art.
